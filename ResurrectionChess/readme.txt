Resurrection Chess
------------------

2 variants
Zillions of Games 2.0 required.

Object: 

Checkmate the opponent's King by attacking it so it cannot escape.
Capturing pieces not cleaned from the board, but can't move.
They are transparent to the all pieces, but may be resurrected by 
friendly pieces.
