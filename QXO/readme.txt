Quantum tic-tac-toe
-------------------
10 variants
Axiomatic

Quantum tic tac toe offers a way of introducing quantum physics without 
mathematics, provides a conceptual foundation for understanding the meaning of 
quantum mechanics, and is fun to play.

This game invented by Allan Goff in 2006.

See also: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quantum_tic-tac-toe
and: https://qttt.googlecode.com/files/QT3-AJP%2010-20-06.pdf
