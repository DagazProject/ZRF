Sokoban Puzzle
--------------
60 variants

Sokoban is a type of transport puzzle, in which the player pushes boxes 
or crates around in a warehouse, trying to get them to storage locations. 
The puzzle is usually implemented as a video game.

Sokoban was created in 1981 by Hiroyuki Imabayashi, and published in 1982 
by Thinking Rabbit, a software house based in Takarazuka, Japan.
