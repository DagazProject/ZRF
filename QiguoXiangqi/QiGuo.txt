Qiguo Xiangqi
-------------
Game of the Seven Kingdoms (Qiguo Xiangqi) is a seven-player variant of the game Xiangqi.
It was invented by Sima Guang (1019-1086).

There are a total of 120 pieces used in this variant
symbolizing the seven Warring States (403-221 B.C.) period.
