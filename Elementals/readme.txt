Elementals
----------
2 variants

It is a war of 100 years of absence of an avatar. Genocide of Fire 
against the Air. Water and Stone are supported by both factions. 
Each fraction has its role in the game:

1. Fire can burn a large number of enemies coming to him
2. Wind can pass through by attacking and change the direction of attack
3. Water can drown enemies or freeze them
4. Stone can push any number of pieces in front

For win, control 4 central squares.
